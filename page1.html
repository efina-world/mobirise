We have lots of lovely Greyhounds here searching for their forever homes and details on each of them can be found in the photo album section of the page. We carry out homechecks before allowing the dogs to leave our care, mainly to check your garden is fully secure all the way round with a minimum 6ft fence and that your home is a safe environment for a Greyhound (we do not do it to see how clean or tidy your house is!) 

We DO rehome to people who work providing they can split the day or have someone who can go in and let the dog in the garden or take them for a walk. (There will need to be someone home during the initial settling in period for the first 1-2 weeks and the time they're left alone will need to be built up gradually. We advise that they are not left for more than 4 hours at any one time)

We DO rehome to people with children of all ages as long as the children are sensible and respect the dogs personal space. Greyhounds are known for being fantastic with children but like any breed they will have a breaking point if tormented by a child. 

Greyhounds make amazing pets and usually adapt very quickly into home life but they are a unique breed so we advise that you do some research into the breed first and of course we are happy to help with any questions you may have. :) 

Although you may see a particular dog on our website or facebook page that you like please come to the kennels with an open mind as we like to try and match the right dog to the right person depending on their circumstances. Every dog in our care is deserving of a loving forever home so please try not to choose on colour but on their individual personality and suitability to your lifestyle. 

All our dogs are neutered, microchipped, vaccinated, will go home with lead, house collar, muzzle, 4 weeks free insurance and a lifetime of support and back up.

Visit Us

You might like the look of a particular dog on our Facebook page, but you will find that the best way to find your new best friend is to go and meet the hounds.
If you already have a dog, our branch volunteers may suggest you take them with you to help you choose.

A home visit

We are dedicated to responsible homing and all adoptions are subject to a home visit. This isn't anything to worry about - we just want to know that our hounds will fit in!

Adoption agreement

Please read through and fill in the adoption agreement. You are then able to take your new loving Greyhound home. (Please note a donation is also required)

We like to keep in touch with all the dogs we home and we do carry out follow up checks. We have a dedicated facebook group where new owners can share photos, stories and ask for / share advise. We are always here for help and support and we also offer a 'holiday' service so that the dogs can come back to stay if you go away. 

If for ANY reason you can't keep the dog whether it be after 2 weeks or 10 years we ALWAYS take them back into our care. This is so we can ensure that they are rehomed to another suitable home with all back up and support in place. 

We rehome mainly to the Essex, Hertfordshire, London, Cambridgeshire, Bedfordshire areas and occasionally further afield.
