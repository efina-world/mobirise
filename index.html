Who are we ?
Barley Kennels (Harlow Branch of Greyhound Trust) is run by a committee of volunteers who all passionately believe that each and every retired greyhound deserves a loving and secure life after racing.

What do we do and why ?
Our main function is to provide the best care for the greyhounds after their racing career has ended and to find the greyhounds the right family home so that they can lead a happy and full life. Greyhounds make the perfect family pet and we want to promote this message far and wide.

Are you interested in a retired greyound as a pet ?
Greyhounds make great pets and are suitable for all kinds of families. Whether you work, have children or other pets, we look at all situations individually and work hard to match the right greyhound with the right family.
